Hive is a virtual community for the University of Waterloo. We want to help connect students at the university with peers and upper year mentors. We hope you'll meet people who have experienced the same sort of troubles that incoming students are encountering and can offer helpful guidance and advice. Our project aims to improve upon existing mentorship initiatives on campus, removing barriers so that students can foster a more enriching experience with the Waterloo community. The goal of the app is to help organically grow these relationships and to allow for longer lasting communication between students by facilitating consistent, in-person interaction.
